Our Motivation:


Due to the rising demand for technology in our industries, it became harder and harder for employees and business managers to keep track of the numbers; especially those who trade in stocks by the hundreds and thousands. That is why we made this application.
To make their lives easier and answer to the rising demands of logistics.
